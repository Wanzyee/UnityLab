Draw multi lines of a UnityEditor.SerializedProperty with children.

First line displays the specified label of the property. Split children into groups, shown as EditorGUI.MultiPropertyField() in lines follow. Looks like UnityEngine.Bounds or UnityEngine.Rect in the Inspector window. Align the line label left as UnityEngine.Transform if exist.
